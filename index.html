BCx Plans

BCx Silver

Unlimited, free, 24/7 physician access for you and your family by phone or video from anywhere in the country.  When a prescription is required BCx provides most of free generic medications commonly prescribed by telemedicine physicians.  BCx pharmacy program is accepted at most pharmacies and provides discounts on many other common medications.

BCx Gold

Includes the Silver program and adds the Individual Assistance Program. Here are just a couple of the items this program provides:

  • Clinical support for emotional wellness, stress management family relationship, pet issues, anxiety, depression, coping, grief, anger management and substance abuse.
  • Legal and Financial Wellness - Telephonic legal assistance for unlimited number of issues per year. Includes one 60-minute in-office or telephonic consultation with a local attorney and 25% discount on continued services. Telephonic financial assistance for unlimited number of issues per year. Includes optional 30-day coaching benefit with 90-day action plan. Identity theft prevention/recovery. Includes 50-minute interactive legal document preparation.

BCx Platinum

Platinum builds on both Silver and Gold and adds access to the First Health national Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) network for both physician and hospital services. Members can take advantage of average physician and hospital discounts ranging from 25%-50%. If you have health insurance coverage, you likely have a PPO network already.
